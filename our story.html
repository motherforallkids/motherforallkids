WHO WE ARE

Mother For All Kids is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing love, support, and hope to children in need. We focus on helping orphans, poor children, sick children, and widows by offering essential resources such as education, healthcare, and nutrition. Our mission is driven by a deep commitment to creating a brighter future for every child, regardless of their circumstances. Through the dedication of our volunteers, donors, and partners, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve. Join us in our journey to spread compassion and transform lives

We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give ...Winston Churchill

WHAT WE DO?

Mother For All Kids! We are a passionate community of individuals committed to making a difference in the lives of children in need. Founded on the principles of compassion and empowerment, our organization focuses on providing essential support to orphans, poor children, sick children, and widows. Through education, healthcare, and nutrition programs, we strive to create a brighter future for every child, regardless of their circumstances. Our dedicated team of volunteers, donors, and supporters work tirelessly to spread love, hope, and joy to those who need it most. Join us in our mission to make a meaningful impact and create positive change in the world

OUR VALUES (CAARE):

  • - Compassion and justice in all of our actions.
  • - Accountability and transparency to all of our stakeholders 2
  • - Action with quality is at the core of our programs. We identify gaps within the communities we serve and implement long-term solutions based on their needs and capacity.
  • - Respect for rights, dignity and equal opportunity of all peoples.
  • - Empower communities and individuals to reach their potential.
