Impact of Your Support

Your Monthly donations are critical for sustaining Mother For All Kids’s pillar programs and maximizing impact in the following:

Health
Mother For All Kids Works with Local Goverment To Immunizes a significant portion of the Country's child population and endeavors to reduce child mortality and disease burden by enhancing the quality of healthcare services and strengthening health systems.
Nutrition
Mother For All Kids delivers lifesaving treatment for severely malnourished children and supports nutrition programs that improve diets and advance best practices for healthy growth and development.
Water
Mother For All Kids helps improve access to safe water for Alot of people in different parts of the country every year while also working to advance innovative solutions to water scarcity and clean water for every home
Education
Every year, Mother For All Kids helps Alot of school children by providing them with the Necessary school requirements so as to get back to learning inorder for them reach their full potential.
Protection
Mother For All Kids partners With Goverment Agencies to protect children from violence both at school and home, exploitation and abuse, and to end harmful practices that violate children’s rights.
